# Brand Reference

> Generated by the deploy-project skill on 2026-04-25. Use this as the source of truth for visual and tonal decisions on this project.

## Brand vibe

Premium-academic, calm and confident. The screen should feel like the lobby of a serious institution — the kind of place where someone reads a journal in a leather chair — not a flashy SaaS dashboard. Deep-forest green grounds the composition; a serif headline does the talking; the rest of the UI gets out of the way.

## Voice and tone

- **Welcoming but unfussy** — "Welcome to Equinim College" not "Hello there!" or "Hey 👋"
- **Concrete and matter-of-fact** — "Room 3 · In Session · Diploma of IT (Cyber Security)" beats "Class is happening!"
- **Australian English** — "colour", "centre", "WA", "Perth time"
- **No emoji in display copy.** Lucide icons handle the visual layer
- **Status words are short and confident** — "Available", "Live", "Break", "In Session"

## Colour palette

Defined as CSS variables in `src/index.css`:

- **Primary green** — `#1a7a54` (`--color-eqc-green`) — used for active states, accents, primary CTAs, the announcement banner
- **Background** — `#062016` (`--color-eqc-bg`) — deep forest, the canvas behind every tile
- **Card / surface** — `#FFFFFF` (`--color-eqc-card`) — every tile sits on white
- **Text** — `#1A1A1A` (`--color-eqc-text`) — near-black for body
- **Muted text** — `#666666` (`--color-eqc-muted`) — labels, secondary info
- **Border** — `#E5E7EB` (`--color-eqc-border`) — subtle hairline borders on cards

Status accents:

- **Live (in session)** — `#1a7a54` (eqc-green) on white text
- **Break** — Tailwind `orange-500` on white text
- **Available** — white card with eqc-green dot
- **Urgent announcement** — Tailwind `red-600`
- **Info announcement** — Tailwind `blue-600`

## Typography

- **Headings & display** — `Cormorant Garamond` (Google Fonts), weights 300–700, italic available. Applied via the `.serif` utility class and to all `h1`/`h2`/`h3`. Tracking is tight on large display numbers (clock).
- **Body & UI** — `Inter` (Google Fonts), full variable range. Default `font-sans` on `body`.
- **Numerals in the clock** — Cormorant Garamond at very large sizes; AM/PM in uppercase.
- **Labels** — Inter, ALL CAPS, tracking `widest`, `font-black` for chips and section eyebrows.

## Design notes

- **Generous whitespace.** Tiles use rounded-2xl / rounded-3xl corners, soft shadows, and ample internal padding. The lobby is read from across the room — nothing should feel cramped.
- **Animated indicators are purposeful.** Pulsing dots mean "live"; the `animate-marquee` banner draws attention without being shouty.
- **Iconography:** Lucide React only. Use the lightest stroke that reads at distance.
- **Photography vs illustration:** Floorplan is illustration; trainer cutouts (in `images/trainer-cutouts/`) are flat PNG cutouts — keep them on white or a soft tint, never on busy backgrounds.
- **Samsung Frame optimisation:** the `@media (min-aspect-ratio: 16/9)` rule adds 5vw side padding for wide-screen lobby displays. Don't remove this.
- **Logo placement:** EQC logo always top-left of the header at `h-20`. Do not stretch or apply colour overlays.
- **Spacing scale:** Tailwind defaults; gaps of 6 (1.5rem) between major sections, 3–4 between within-card elements.
